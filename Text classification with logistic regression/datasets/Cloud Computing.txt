  Title:   The Role of Cloud Computing in AI  
  Content:    
Cloud computing has become a crucial enabler of AI, providing scalable infrastructure, data storage, and computing power. With cloud services like AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud, businesses can access machine learning frameworks, manage big data, and train complex AI models without maintaining their own infrastructure.  
The elasticity of cloud platforms allows companies to scale resources up or down as needed, making them cost-effective for both startups and large enterprises. Cloud computing has accelerated AI innovation by democratizing access to powerful computational tools.
